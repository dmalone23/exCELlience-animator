-Textual view outputs a textual representation in the same format from
the assignment page of previous assignments.
-The interactive view has 6 buttons.
  -Play button that starts the animation as well as starting the animation off of a pause button press.
  -Pause button that pauses the timer of the animation until it is started again by the play button.
  -SpeedUp button that speeds up the animation.
  -SlowDown button that slows the speed of the animation.
  -Restart button that restarts the animation.
  -Looping button that enables and disables looping.
